OSV-Scanner is a vulnerability scanner by Google that uses the OSV.dev database.

It supports scanning source directories, container images, and lockfiles across 11+ ecosystems (Go, Java, Python, Node, etc.). Features include guided remediation, license scanning, and offline mode.

A powerful tool for maintaining open source supply chain security.
