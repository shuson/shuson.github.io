freeCodeCamp continues to dominate as the #1 trending repository on GitHub today — the world's largest open-source codebase and curriculum for learning math, programming, and computer science, completely free.

With a staggering **448,545 ⭐ stars** (+633 today) and over 45,000 forks, freeCodeCamp has grown into the definitive starting point for aspiring developers worldwide. Built primarily in TypeScript with React, Node.js, and D3.js, the platform offers thousands of hours of interactive coding challenges, projects, and certifications across web development, data science, and machine learning — all while contributing to real-world nonprofit projects.

It's a rare open-source project that is simultaneously a technical education platform and a massive community movement. URL: https://github.com/freeCodeCamp/freeCodeCamp
