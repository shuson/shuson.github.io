headroom is a Python toolkit that dramatically compresses tool outputs, logs, files, and RAG chunks before they reach the LLM — saving 60-95% on tokens while maintaining answer quality. It offers a library, a proxy, and an MCP server, making it easy to plug into Claude Code, Cursor, LangChain, and other agent workflows. With built-in smart truncation, deduplication, and structure-preserving compression, headroom tackles one of the biggest cost and latency bottlenecks in LLM-powered applications.
