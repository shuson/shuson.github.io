Google Research's TimesFM (Time Series Foundation Model) is a pretrained foundation model for time-series forecasting, bringing the transformer revolution to temporal data.

Built in Python and boasting 23,139 ⭐ (+844 today), TimesFM treats forecasting as a general-purpose task — learn once from massive cross-domain data, then zero-shot or fine-tune on your own series. It's a compelling alternative to traditional ARIMA or Prophet pipelines, with Google-scale pretraining behind it.

As foundation models continue to expand beyond text and images, TimesFM signals that time-series is next in line for the pretrain-and-adapt paradigm. URL: https://github.com/google-research/timesfm
